## When to Contact Whom

| Question Type | Contact |
|---------------|---------|
| "Can I build X on my property?" | Planning Division (county) or City Planning |
| "What permits do I need?" | Building Division (county) or City Building |
| "What's my property tax?" | Assessor's Office |
| "Who owns this property?" | Assessor-Recorder's Office |
| "Is my property in a flood zone?" | FEMA or Planning Division |
| "Is my property in a fire hazard zone?" | CAL FIRE or local fire district |
| "Can I install a septic system?" | Environmental Health |
| "Who is my supervisor?" | Clerk of the Board |
| "I have a county road issue" | Public Works |
