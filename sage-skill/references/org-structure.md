# Solano County Organizational Structure

Quick reference for understanding county government organization, departments, and leadership.

## Overall Structure

**Total Employees**: ~3,285 FTEs (FY2025/26)
**Total Budget**: ~$1.65 billion

The county organization flows from the **Electorate** who elect:
- Board of Supervisors (5 districts)
- Constitutional Officers (Sheriff, DA, Assessor, etc.)

The Board appoints the **County Administrator** who oversees appointed department heads.

## Understanding Department Naming

- **"Resource Management"** = Planning + Building + Environmental Health + Parks
- **"H&SS"** = Health & Social Services (the mega-department)
- **"DoIT"** = Department of Information Technology (also runs Registrar of Voters)
- **"IHSS"** = In-Home Supportive Services (home care for elderly/disabled)
- **"E&ES"** = Employment & Eligibility Services (CalWORKs, Medi-Cal enrollment)

## Reporting Relationships

```
Electorate
├── Board of Supervisors (5 elected)
│   └── County Administrator (appointed)
│       ├── Assistant County Administrators (2)
│       └── Appointed Department Heads
│           ├── H&SS Director
│           ├── Resource Management Director
│           ├── General Services Director
│           ├── Human Resources Director
│           ├── IT Director
│           ├── Library Director
│           ├── Probation Chief
│           ├── County Counsel
│           └── (others)
│
└── Constitutional Officers (6 elected)
    ├── Sheriff/Coroner
    ├── District Attorney
    ├── Public Defender
    ├── Assessor/Recorder
    ├── Auditor-Controller
    └── Treasurer/Tax Collector/County Clerk
```

*Note: Constitutional officers are elected independently and do not report to the County Administrator, though they coordinate with county leadership on budget and policy matters.*
